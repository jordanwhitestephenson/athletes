ABOUT GIRO

Founded in Santa Cruz, California in 1985, Giro Sport Design is a leader in the design, fit and engineering of innovative products that complement the body and enhance the rider’s experience. From the first lightweight, high-performance helmets for cycling and snowsports, to category-leading apparel, footwear and accessories, Giro is committed to being the first choice of riders because we understand that riding is the best part of a great life.

    OUR OFFICES

    SANTA CRUZ, CA

    Giro comes from a unique place. Santa Cruz’s location, at the intersection of surf and mountains, attracts people of an independent mindset; outdoors people; iconoclasts who set their own priorities. Counterintuitively, it turns out that Santa Cruz, a place where business shuts down when the surf is up, has proven to be a powerful entrepreneurial incubator. This be-your own-boss mentality, anarchic to the corporate status quo, is the engine that drives today’s counter-culture to becoming tomorrow’s culture. We think this is a good place to be.

    PARK CITY, UT

    Located in the heart of the Wasatch section of the Rocky Mountains and within minutes of three world-class mountain resorts, our Park City office is an ideal base for creating equipment to enhance the riding experience. From epic riding and scenery, to leading arts, culture and entertainment, Park City fuels the spirit and the imagination while providing us with a direct connection to the riders and the culture that we serve on a global scale. We think of it as an ideal laboratory for testing gear, and a great place to call home.
